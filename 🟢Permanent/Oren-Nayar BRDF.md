# Oren-Nayar BRDF

## Introduction

- this is the most common rough diffuse [[Bidirectional Scattering Distribution Function (BSDF) | BRDF]] 
- basicly a [[Microfacet Models]] with diffuse microfacets

- characteristic effect of rough diffuse BRDFs is <mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;">retroreflection</mark> 
	- the shadow/masking term makes the rim very bright (in comparison to [[Lambert Diffuse]])
