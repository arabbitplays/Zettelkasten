# Wave II - Architecture-based Adaptation

## Introduction

- happened from about 2004 to 2012
- there was a lack of principled engineering
	- defined architectures allow to define self-adaptive systems and reason about their adaptation at runtime
- the loop of change shall be externalized to allow for better separation of concerns, reuse, monitoring, ...

## 3-Layer Model for Self-Adaptation

- we have
	- <mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;">component control</mark> - functionality of the application
	- <mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;">change management</mark> - execute changes in the lower layer based on the plans
	- <mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;">goal management</mark> - re-plan and deal with changes and new goals
