# von Mises Fisher Distribution

## Introduction

- distribution on the sphere characterized by a mean direction $\mu\ (||\mu|| = 1)$ and a concentration parameter $\kappa$ $$f(x, \mu, \kappa) = \frac{\kappa}{2\pi(e^\kappa - e^{-\kappa})}exp(\kappa \mu^\top x)$$
